ABOUT PETmalu

PETmalu is a fun and interactive platform created for pet lovers who want to enjoy caring for their pets while connecting with other fur parents. Our goal is to make pet care exciting, rewarding, and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to create a friendly online community where newly pet owners can learn, share experiences, and enjoy activities with their pets through games, rewards, and exciting features.
